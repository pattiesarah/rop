Bayan System

An electronic customs data system for all customs procedures and operations enabling customers to finalize all customs interactions electronically.

Bayan system components

  • Registration of commercial partner system: through providing a code for per partner.
  • Manifest system: to connect systems of air, marine cargo by customs system.
  • Customs declaration registration: which considers an application and acknowledge from the trader showing types of goods that he wants to import or export.
  • Estimating taxes and fees system: calculating due fees and taxes.
  • Refunding system: to refund undue taxes.
  • Finance affairs systems: to manage finance processes and affairs.
  • Exemption system: to monitor customs exemptions.
  • Cottas management system: to control goods with limited cottas.
  • Guarantee management system: to control different types of guarantees.
  • Customs value system: to monitor values provided comparing them with existing values at the data base.
  • Customs tariff system: to identify terms of goods tariffs.
  • Inspection system: inspection.
  • Mobile application system: to send inspection results.
  • Goods releasing system: to release goods completed procedures.
  • Departure permit system: to issue a departure permit.
  • Arrival registration system: registration of carrier information.
  • Vehicles movement management system: registration of exit/entering of vehicles.
  • Issues system: to register and follow up issues and judgments.
  • System management: codes management.
  • Economic operator system: golden list.
  • Customs warehouses system: to control what is entering and exiting from them.
  • Customs auditing system: to audit all financial and procedures of all customs transactions.
  • Free zones system: to control imported and exported goods.
  • Auctions system: to sell neglected and confiscated goods.
  • Supporting decision making system: to provide with information.
  • Statistics reporting system: to organize and issuing statistics reports.

Aims of the system

  • To reinforce control over security, health and environment of imports and exports, re-export, and transit goods.
  • Connecting all bodies of public/private sectors with customs and exchanging information electronically via single electronic window.
  • Computerization of all customs procedures and financial, banking processes.
  • Decreasing expenses of importation and exportation operations.
  • To up scaling the level of the Sultanate of Oman at the international circles in the trading and economic aspects.
  • To encourage importers and exporters to use Omani seaports.
  • Encourage national and international investments.
  • Provide rapid facilitation and electronic services for trade community in real time.
  • Present accurate statistics, and analysis for international trade to enable investors and trade project owners to prepare realistic economic feasibility studies.
  • To combat trade counterfeiting.
  • To establish electronic data base for all customs, security, health, and environment \risks and others.
  • Unifying customs procedures to be achieved in less time.
  • To limit using paper documents.
  • To apply right roles of value agreement avoiding disagreements between importers and customs officers at estimating customs values.
  • Statistics information exactness.
  • Providing detailed information of all imported and exported goods.
  • Quick providing and publication of statistics information.
  • Analyze information and identifying statistics and economic expectations indicators.
  • Using information outcomes in fields of concerned studies and researches.
  • To ease comparisons between Oman exports and that of the neighboring countries.
  • Present accurate information for feasibility studies of investors.
  • To encourage foreign and local investments to establish local industries for imported materials.
  • Support auditing of customs transactions processes , collecting due customs taxes.
  • Approve the real value of goods according to international value agreement.
  • Providing preferential information about all types of customs exemptions and revenues.
  • Supporting customs refunding system and guarantees.
  • Issuing licenses and permits electronically to facilitate and fastening consignments releasing.
  • Stop using paper documents which limit and stop case of falsification.
  • Pre-arrival information electronically will help to audit and identifying risks, in addition to shortening procedures.
  • Working with electronic manifest between directorate general of customs and fright agents and establishments work at seaports and airport services.

Bayan system – it's importance

  • To dispense with paper- documents.
  • No need to liaise separately with relevant bodies.
  • Possibility of pre- arrival releasing.
  • Unifying procedures, equality and transparency in treatment.
  • Electronic payments.
  • Risk management.
  • Access to accurate statistical data and information.
  • Avoids repetition of data.
  • Saves time, effort and money.
  • To raise economic status of Sultanate of Oman.
